# Metadata for Curtogrowth_data.csv
- **Incubation Period:** 1/22/2025 to 3/5/2025

### Identifiers
- **Treatment:** Experimental group (Neg: Control, D26: Inoculated, G58: Inoculated).
- **Replicate:** Replicate number (1-4) within each treatment.
- **Collection_point:** Sequential sampling point ID.
- **SampleID:** Unique identifier for each sample.
- **bagid:** Unique identifier for each bag (1-12) used for longitudinal tracking.
- **Date:** Date of sampling (MM/DD/YYYY).

### Sample Properties
- **proportion_dry:** Dry mass ratio (used for correction).
- **mass_to_cell_extraction:** Initial mass used for extraction (g).

### Plating (Colony) Data - Raw Counts
- **Colony_count_0** to **Colony_count_10-5:** Raw colony counts at different dilution factors (0 to 10^-5).

### Plating (Colony) Data - Corrected
- **Colony_corrected_0** to **Colony_corrected_10-5:** Colony counts corrected per gram of dry litter and for the respective dilution factor. (Cells/g dry litter).

### Flow Cytometry Data - Corrected
- **E4_gate_dilution_drymass_corrected:** Cell count from Flow Cytometry Gate E4, corrected per gram of dry litter and dilution.
- **E5_gate_dilution_drymass_corrected:** Cell count from Flow Cytometry Gate E5, corrected per gram of dry litter and dilution.
- **E6_gate_dilution_drymass_corrected:** Cell count from Flow Cytometry Gate E6, corrected per gram of dry litter and dilution.

## Data Notes
- Non-numeric values like `#VALUE!`, `n`, `full`, or `incontable` indicate missing data or counts that were outside the measurable range.
